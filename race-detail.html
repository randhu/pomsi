    National winner Belgium Yearlings: Bonte Dirk (Rijmenam)

    The National winner Belgium from Bourges I 2020 in the category "Yearlings" is Bonte Dirk (Rijmenam).
    The pigeon was clocked on saturday at 14:22 CEST after a race of 465.11 km, achieving an average velocity of 1805.47 m/min.

    National winner old pigeons: Jan Moors (Zonhoven)

    The National winner Belgium from Bourges I in the category "old birds" is Jan Moors (Zonhoven).
    The pigeon was clocked on Saturday at 14:28:00 CEST after a race of 485.64 km, achieving an average velocity of 1846,54 m/min.
